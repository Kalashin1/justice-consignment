Frequently Ask Questions

How do I get a quote for my shipment?

Getting a quote is easy! Simply visit our website and fill out our online quote form. You can also contact us directly by phone or email, and our friendly customer support team will be happy to assist you.

What information do I need to get a quote?

To provide the most accurate quote, we'll need details like the origin and destination of your shipment, the type and weight of your goods, and your preferred delivery timeframe.

How can I track my shipment?

We offer real-time shipment tracking through our user-friendly online portal or mobile app. Simply enter your shipment reference number, and you'll see the current location and status of your shipment.

What happens if my shipment is damaged during transport?

We take pride in careful handling and secure transport. However, in the unlikely event of damage, please contact us immediately. We have a clear claims process in place to ensure a prompt resolution.

Can I change the delivery address for my shipment?

In some cases, we may be able to accommodate address changes before the shipment arrives at the original destination. Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your options and any potential fees.

How do I cancel a shipment?

We understand that plans can change. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel a shipment. Cancellation fees may apply depending on the stage of the shipping process.

How can I get a refund for my shipment?

Our refund policy is clearly outlined in our terms and conditions. Generally, refunds are offered for shipment cancellations within a specific timeframe or for damages exceeding a certain value. Please contact customer support for specific details related to your situation.

What are your payment options?

We offer a variety of secure payment options for your convenience, including credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. The specific options available may vary depending on your location and shipment details.
